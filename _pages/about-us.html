--- title: About us date: 2018-10-23 22:42:53 +0200 layout: page order: 2 description: 'House of Worship Ministry commenced with their first church service on the 21st of July 2013 at No. 7 Loop Street, Cape Town with General Overseer Pastor Jeff Onyems and his wife. God added to their number and in April 2014 the church moved to her second location at 8 Rochester Street Observatory with 9 dedicated members. Ever since then God has worked mightily through the General Overseer Pastor Jeff Onyems as lots of miracles happened. More souls has been added consistently over the years which led to the relocation of the church to a new church premises at No. 48 Hopkins Street Salt River Cape Town on 19th December 2014. At the church premises in Salt River great multitudes joined the ministry within 3 to 4 years through the prophetic anointing of the Man of God Pastor Jeff Onyems and his assistants. The ministry rose to over 100 dedicated members and this meant that a new church premises with a larger venue was needed to accommodate the growing number of church members. God through one of the church members made the vision possible as he helped facilitate the new and current venue of the church which is 19 Spencer Road Salt River Cape Town South Africa. The church oficially moved into the new premises on the 28th March 2018 and the first Sunday service was held on the 1st of April 2018.
